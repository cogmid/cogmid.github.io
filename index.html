Corin Middleton is a multidisciplinary artist and designer living and working in Bristol, UK. He graduated from The University of the West of England with a BA in Graphic Design in 2022. Through his work Corin explores niche and strange topics, focusing heavily on processes and the physicality of his outcomes.
